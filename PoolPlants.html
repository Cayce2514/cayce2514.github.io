Poolside Trees – Zone 9b

Cordyline (Ti Plant)

Cordyline spp.

Mature Size
Approx. 3–10 ft tall × 3–8 ft wide (varies by cultivar)
Type
Evergreen (in mild climates)
Sunlight
Full sun to partial shade; in hot inland climates, morning sun with afternoon shade is ideal
Watering
Moderate; keep evenly moist while establishing, then allow the topsoil to dry slightly between waterings
Fertilizer
Balanced slow-release fertilizer in spring; optional light summer feeding if growth is weak
Zone 9b Notes
Performs well in Zone 9b when protected from frost and harsh afternoon sun; good for somewhat sheltered poolside locations

Dracaena

Dracaena spp.

Mature Size
Approx. 6–15 ft tall × 3–8 ft wide (varies by species/cultivar)
Type
Evergreen
Sunlight
Bright light; full sun to light shade outdoors; avoid extremely hot, reflected sun from hardscape
Watering
Moderate; prefers well-drained soil and should not be kept constantly soggy
Fertilizer
Light applications of a balanced fertilizer in spring and early summer
Zone 9b Notes
Many Dracaena species do well in Zone 9b with only light frost; best in protected spots near walls, fences, or patios

Abyssinian Banana

Ensete ventricosum

Mature Size
Approx. 10–20 ft tall × 8–12 ft wide
Type
Evergreen in frost-free areas; foliage is frost-tender
Sunlight
Full sun to light shade; some afternoon shade helpful in very hot inland sites
Watering
High; needs regular deep watering, especially in hot weather, with well-drained but moisture-retentive soil
Fertilizer
Heavy feeder; benefits from rich soil and frequent fertilization during the growing season
Zone 9b Notes
Can be grown in Zone 9b with winter protection; foliage is likely to be frost-damaged in cold winters but plants often resprout from the base

Bronze Loquat

Eriobotrya deflexa

Mature Size
Approx. 15–25 ft tall × 15–25 ft wide
Type
Evergreen
Sunlight
Full sun to light shade
Watering
Moderate; regular water until established, then somewhat drought tolerant with occasional deep irrigation
Fertilizer
Balanced fertilizer in spring; generally not a heavy feeder
Zone 9b Notes
Well suited to Zone 9b; handles heat well and provides dense broadleaf evergreen foliage for poolside shade/backdrop

Tree Ferns

Dicksonia spp., Cyathea spp.

Mature Size
Often 10–20 ft tall over time; crown spread approx. 6–10 ft
Type
Evergreen in mild climates
Sunlight
Bright shade to dappled light; avoid full hot sun and drying winds
Watering
High; require consistently moist soil and good ambient humidity, with particular attention to watering the crown
Fertilizer
Light, regular feeding during the growing season with balanced or organic fertilizer
Zone 9b Notes
Marginal but possible in sheltered, shaded, irrigated microclimates; less suited to exposed, hot, dry pool decks

Elephant Ear Fig

Ficus auriculata

Mature Size
Approx. 15–30+ ft tall × 15–30+ ft wide
Type
Evergreen to semi-evergreen in mild climates
Sunlight
Full sun to part shade
Watering
Moderate; prefers regular moisture, especially when young
Fertilizer
Balanced fertilizer in spring; can benefit from additional feeding in poor soils
Zone 9b Notes
Generally suitable for warm climates like Zone 9b, but eventual size and vigorous roots mean it’s better placed away from the immediate pool edge

Australian Willow

Geijera parviflora

Mature Size
Approx. 20–35 ft tall × 15–20 ft wide
Type
Evergreen
Sunlight
Full sun
Watering
Low to moderate; drought tolerant once established
Fertilizer
Light feeding in spring; performs well with minimal fertilization
Zone 9b Notes
Very well suited to hot, dry conditions in Zone 9b; fine-textured foliage and narrow habit make it an excellent low-litter poolside tree

Ornamental Banana

Musa spp.

Mature Size
Typically 6–15 ft tall × 6–12 ft wide (depending on species/cultivar)
Type
Herbaceous perennial; above-ground foliage is frost-tender
Sunlight
Full sun to part sun; afternoon shade can reduce leaf scorch in hot inland locations
Watering
High; bananas are thirsty and need regular deep watering in the growing season
Fertilizer
Heavy feeders; benefit from frequent fertilization (e.g., monthly) during active growth
Zone 9b Notes
Performs well in Zone 9b for a strong tropical effect; expect foliage damage and possible die-back in cold winters, but clumps usually resprout

Pool-suitable Palms

Various genera

Mature Size
Many pool-appropriate palms range roughly 10–30 ft tall × 8–15 ft wide
Type
Evergreen
Sunlight
Generally full sun; some species tolerate or prefer partial shade
Watering
Moderate; many are drought tolerant once established but look best with occasional deep watering
Fertilizer
Palm-specific or balanced fertilizer in spring and summer, often with micronutrients
Zone 9b Notes
Many palm species perform very well in Zone 9b and are classic poolside plants; choose non-invasive, relatively clean species with minimal fruit and frond drop

Majestic Beauty Indian Hawthorn

Rhaphiolepis ‘Majestic Beauty’

Mature Size
Often trained as a small tree approx. 8–12 ft tall × 6–10 ft wide
Type
Evergreen
Sunlight
Full sun to light shade
Watering
Low to moderate; tolerant of some drought once established
Fertilizer
Light feeding in spring with a balanced fertilizer
Zone 9b Notes
Very well adapted to Zone 9b; neat evergreen foliage and spring flowers with relatively modest litter make it a good choice near pools

Schefflera

Schefflera spp.

Mature Size
Tree forms often 10–30 ft tall × 8–15 ft wide (species dependent)
Type
Evergreen
Sunlight
Bright filtered light to part sun; can handle more sun in milder/coastal locations
Watering
Moderate; prefers evenly moist but well-drained soil
Fertilizer
Balanced fertilizer in spring and mid-summer
Zone 9b Notes
Can grow vigorously in Zone 9b and provide lush tropical foliage; confirm local guidance, as some species are invasive in certain regions

Golden Trumpet Tree

Tabebuia chrysotricha

Mature Size
Approx. 20–30 ft tall × 15–25 ft wide
Type
Semi-deciduous to deciduous (often dropping leaves just before flowering)
Sunlight
Full sun
Watering
Moderate; regular deep watering while establishing, then somewhat drought tolerant
Fertilizer
Balanced fertilizer in spring; not a heavy feeder
Zone 9b Notes
Well suited to warm climates such as Zone 9b; produces spectacular golden-yellow flowers in spring, with some leaf and flower litter to consider near pools
